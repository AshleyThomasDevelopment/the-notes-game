Play The Arnold Game!

Instructions:

At the beginning of each turn a sound will play. It is your job to repeat the pattern back. On each new turn you will only see/hear Arnold's new pick, while you still have to input the entire pattern from the beginning. Don't take Arnold too seriously. Good Luck!

Limit Meter:

Depending on how quickly you are able to complete Arnold's pattern, your meter may fill. Once it reaches the top you have a choice of two special powers to use whenever you wish. BONUS: If your meter is filled when you lose, you carry the powers over to the next game. You can have only one extra power per game.

Options:

If you want an extra challenge, "Party Mode" will continually change the colors of the squares, and get faster with every turn. Some items on the screen can be double-clicked to make their color slowly change. Single-click to stop it. Customize your game!

Tips:

We humans are good at remembering small chunks of information. Use this to your advantage! Try remembering the pattern in smaller groups of 2, 3, or even 5!
